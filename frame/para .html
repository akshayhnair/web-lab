Mohandas college engineering and technology(mcet) is an engineering and technology college located at the village anad, trivandrum district, kerala india.the college,which is affliated to the KTU,was established in 2002.Mohandas college is a project of the mohandas group of companies and is manged by the VN GANGADHARA PANIKKER MEMRIAL TRUST. the college has recieved ISO 9001:2008 certification
